What We Offer

Mongis Works offers a comprehensive range of services, including:

Design and Planning

Our design and planning expertise ensures that your projects are meticulously planned and thoughtfully designed. We produce high-quality, locally made designs that exhibit skilled craftsmanship and could increase value and exclusivity of the product itself.

Project Management

Our project management team understands that materiality is everything. We meticulously select top-notch materials and provide the final aesthetic touch to ensure your projects are executed with precision and efficiency.

Construction and Implementation

Our construction and implementation experts elevate your spaces through creativity and craftsmanship. We bring iconic design products to life, following the latest trends and ensuring your projects are executed with excellence.

Quality Control and Assurance

Quality is at the core of our operations. We trust our skilled woodworkers to achieve targeted quality levels and pay meticulous attention to every detail, from construction to finish, to deliver the highest quality results for your projects.

Environmental Compliance

Environmental compliance is a top priority for us. All our products are packed safely according to transportation requirements and delivered promptly to your doorstep. But, of course, if the item is in stock, feel free to pick up the product at our warehouse right away.
